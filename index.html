the "reputation" album

Taylor Swift's sixth studio album "reputation" was released November 10th, 2017. It featured singles such as "look what you made me do" and "delicate." The album has been the recipient of multiple accolades such as the American Music Award for Favorite Pop/Rock Album and the Billboard Music Award for Top Selling Album.

netflix tour film

The Reputation Stadium Tour began May 8, 2018 and ended on November 21, 2018. The tour's last show in the United States, which was in Dallas, TX, was filmed and later released on Netlifx on December 31st, 2018. The "Reputation Stadium Tour" became the highest grossing tour ever in the United States and North America.

the making of "reputation"

"The Making Of a Song," is a series featuring behind the scenes content of the making of "reputation". AT&T put out the project due to a contract Swift signed with the company during that time. The album "reputation" features 15 songs, but only 8/15 songs on the album got an episode for the "making of" series.
